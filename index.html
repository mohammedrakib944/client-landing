ABOUT

The Game

DoRac is a metaverse space crypto play-to-earn game that will offer a few ways on how you as a player or just a visitor can participate in it. Starting with raising your own dogs, breeding them, and training them to perform better in competitions and races.
As a company we want to provide our users with entertaining metaverse where they can have fun and earn money doing it. Exciting upgrades will come along the platform growth.

DECENTRALISED ECONOMY

Our Native Token $DORC

Our Native Token DORC is DEFLATIONARY and built on Binance smart chain, you can use it to participate in races, train, feed or use it for any other amazing services we have planned for your dogs. You can read more about tokenomics in our whitepaper.

$DRT Token Utility

This token will serve as free race or daily $DRT race reward and will be mostly used in combination with $DORC token to cover breeding fees, will also be used to partly pay for other services and consumables in DoRac metaverse.
